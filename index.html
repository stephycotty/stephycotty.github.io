Music Genre Classification

In this project, I developed a hybrid deep learning model combining Convolutional Neural Networks (CNN) with a Swin Transformer to classify music genres from spectrogram images. The CNN captured local spatial features, while the Swin Transformer leveraged attention mechanisms to model long-range dependencies and global patterns. This hybrid architecture significantly improved genre classification accuracy compared to baseline models, demonstrating the effectiveness of combining convolutional and transformer-based approaches for audio-based image recognition tasks.
